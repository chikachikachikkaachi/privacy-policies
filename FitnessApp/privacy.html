1. Data We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services, including sensitive information related to your health and body measurements, as well as data necessary for advertising.

A. Personal Data Provided Directly by the User:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password (hashed), and unique user ID.
  • Health and Fitness Data: Target weight, current weight, height, and fitness goals.
  • Sensitive Body Image Data (User-Generated Content):
    • Photographs: Images of the user's body uploaded for analysis.
    • AI Analysis Data: The results and numerical body measurements derived by our AI from the user's photographs.

B. Automatically Collected Data (Usage Data):

When you use the App, we automatically collect data about your device and how you interact with the App. This includes:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID, IDFA).
  • Usage Data: App screens viewed, features accessed, time spent on the App, and crash logs.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your collected data for the following primary purposes:

  • To Provide Core Service: To use the AI to analyze your body photographs and generate personalized fitness process plans and metrics.
  • To Personalize Experience: To track your progress, display relevant milestones, and customize the App interface.
  • For Communication: To send you notifications about your progress, account updates, and essential service messages.
  • For Analytics and Improvement: To understand how Users interact with the App (e.g., which AI features are used most) to identify bugs and improve functionality.
  • For Advertising (See Section 3): To deliver targeted advertisements to you.

4. Data Storage and Security

A. Data Processing and Storage (On-Device)

Crucially, we do not store your sensitive body images or the AI analysis data on any of our servers.

  • AI Analysis (On-Device Only): All photographic processing and AI-based analysis happen entirely on your **local device** (i.e., your smartphone) using our in-house model. **Your photos are NEVER uploaded to our cloud servers or to any third-party servers.**
  • Third-Party Data Sharing: We confirm that the AI analysis process does **NOT** involve transferring your photographic data to any third-party for storage or processing.
  • Data Retention: The resulting fitness metrics and your personalized process plan are stored **only on your device** unless you explicitly use a cloud backup feature (which you control).
  • Server Data: The only data we store on our secure servers is the minimal **Account Information** (Name, email, hashed password) required for login and Google AdMob-related Usage Data (see Section 3).

B. Security:

We implement appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect. However, no electronic transmission or storage method is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
